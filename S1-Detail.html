COMME des GARÇONS PLAY — COMME des GARÇONS PLAY and Converse are bringing their beloved heart to a different silhouette: the One Star. Design-wise, the collaboration is nothing groundbreaking, preferring simplicity over gaudy overuse of said logo. On the exterior of both colorways — “Black” and “White” — the Filip Pagowski-designed graphic is printed next to the lateral star, matching the shapes bright red color at a rotation of 90 degrees counterclockwise.
